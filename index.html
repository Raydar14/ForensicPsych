Scope of Services

Core forensic evaluation services

Each evaluation is tailored to the specific legal question, with methods and reporting prepared to meet the standards of admissibility.

Competency

Competency to Proceed

Determining an individual's current cognitive and psychological capacity to understand the nature and object of the proceedings and to assist effectively in their own defense.

Responsibility

Criminal Responsibility

Assessing mental state at the time of the alleged offense to determine whether a mental disease or defect precluded understanding the wrongfulness of the conduct, or conforming conduct to the law.

Risk

Violence Risk Assessment

A structured, professional evaluation of the likelihood of future violent or criminal behavior, examining historical, clinical, and contextual risk factors.

Mitigation

Mitigation & Sentencing

Identifying and analyzing clinical factors — trauma history, mental illness, intellectual disability — that may serve as mitigating evidence to inform sentencing and disposition.
